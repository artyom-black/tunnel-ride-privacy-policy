1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you install or play Tunnel Ride (the “Game”).

Tunnel Ride is a mobile game that may use third-party platform and advertising services to support distribution, functionality, analytics, and ad delivery. Some information is stored locally on your device, while some information may be processed by third-party service providers.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your device, platform, and the services active in the Game, the following categories of information may be collected or processed:

  • Device and technical information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, general diagnostic information, and crash or performance data.
  • Gameplay and local data: game settings, progress, scores, unlocks, and similar gameplay data stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising and identifier data: IP address, advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers, ad interactions, and basic app interaction data that may be processed by advertising or platform providers.
Tunnel Ride does not require you to create an account to play. If you contact the developer directly by email, the information you provide in that message will be used only to respond to your request.

3. How We Use Information

Information handled in connection with the Game may be used to:

  • operate, maintain, and deliver the Game,
  • store gameplay progress and user preferences on your device,
  • improve stability, performance, and gameplay quality,
  • identify, diagnose, and fix technical issues,
  • serve and measure advertising where ads are enabled, and
  • respond to support or privacy-related inquiries sent to the developer.

4. Third-Party Services

Tunnel Ride may use third-party services and SDKs, including services provided by Google Play and Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads, for app distribution, platform functionality, and advertising.

These third-party services may collect or process information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms. Their data practices are separate from this website and may include the collection of device information, identifiers, IP address, diagnostic data, and app interaction data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay progress, settings, and similar local data stored by the Game generally remain on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear the app’s data through your device settings.

Information processed by third-party service providers is retained in accordance with their own retention practices and legal obligations. If you have a question about data handling or would like to make a privacy-related request, you may contact the developer using the email address listed below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tunnel Ride is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age required by applicable law without appropriate authorization. If you believe that personal information has been provided improperly, please contact the developer so the matter can be reviewed.

7. Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to help protect information related to the Game. However, no method of electronic storage, transmission, or processing can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Choices

Depending on your device and platform settings, you may be able to:

  • uninstall the Game at any time,
  • clear locally stored app data through your device settings,
  • manage advertising and privacy preferences available on your device or Google account, and
  • contact the developer with privacy-related questions or requests.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page together with a revised effective date.
