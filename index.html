1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed when you install, access, or play Tunnel Ride (the “Game”).

Tunnel Ride requires an active internet connection to start and play. In connection with gameplay, app distribution, platform functionality, and advertising, certain information may be stored locally on your device and certain information may be processed by third-party service providers.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on your device, platform, and the services used in connection with the Game, the following categories of information may be collected or processed:

  • Device and technical information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, general diagnostic information, and crash, stability, or performance data.
  • Gameplay and local data: game settings, progress, scores, unlocks, and similar gameplay information stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising and identifier data: IP address, advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers, ad interactions, and basic app interaction data that may be processed by advertising or platform providers.
  • Connectivity verification data: the Game may send a lightweight network request to verify whether an active internet connection is available before gameplay begins.
Tunnel Ride does not require you to create a user account. If you contact the developer directly by email, the information you provide in your message will be used only to respond to your request.

3. How We Use Information

Information handled in connection with the Game may be used to:

  • operate, maintain, and provide the Game,
  • verify internet connectivity and determine whether access to the Game can be provided,
  • store gameplay progress and user preferences on your device,
  • improve stability, performance, and gameplay quality,
  • identify, diagnose, and resolve technical issues,
  • serve and measure advertising where ads are enabled, and
  • respond to support or privacy-related inquiries sent to the developer.

4. Third-Party Services

Tunnel Ride may use third-party services and SDKs, including Google Play services and Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads, for app distribution, platform functionality, and advertising.

These third-party services may collect or process information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms. Their data practices are independent from this website and may include the processing of device information, identifiers, IP address, diagnostic data, advertising data, and app interaction data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay progress, settings, and similar local data stored by the Game generally remain on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear the app’s data through your device settings.

Information processed by third-party service providers is retained in accordance with their own retention practices, legal obligations, and operational requirements. If you have a question about data handling or would like to make a privacy-related request, you may contact the developer using the email address listed below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tunnel Ride is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children below the age required by applicable law without appropriate authorization. If you believe that personal information may have been provided improperly, please contact the developer so the matter can be reviewed.

7. Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to help protect information related to the Game. However, no method of electronic storage, transmission, or processing can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Choices

Depending on your device and platform settings, you may be able to:

  • uninstall the Game at any time,
  • clear locally stored app data through your device settings,
  • manage advertising and privacy preferences available on your device or Google account,
  • choose whether to contact the developer with privacy-related questions or requests, and
  • stop using the Game if you do not want to use a service that requires an active internet connection.
